About Us

Koila Multi Cuisine Restaurant is located in Sorakhutte,Sankhamul and Gatthaghar,Nepal. The restaurant offers a diverse menu that features a variety of culinary traditions, including Nepali, Indian,and continental dishes. They focus on using fresh ingredients and authentic spices to create flavorful dishes.Additionally, Koila offers delivery options, allowing customers to enjoy their meals home.
